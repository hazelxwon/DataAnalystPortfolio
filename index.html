Data Analyst Portfolio
by Hazel Won.

Hi there! Welcome to my portfolio page. I'm a certified data analyst based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I'm passionate about all things data and using data to unlock potential - from my experience as an educator using student achievement data to unlock learning potential, to being a Data Analyst Fellow unlocking revenue potential of businesses. These are a few of my projects. Feel free to explore!

Instacart Product Analysis

JuPyter Notebook exploratory data analysis using Python (numpy, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn). Analysis of big data (3 million+ records) to create actionable business recommendations to promote strategic products.

Instacart Product Analysis Presentation

Executive Presentation with Tableau visualizations to tell compelling data story and insights for actionable business solutions.

Hate Crimes in America

Interactive Tableau dashboards visualizing hate crime incidences 1990-2020. Data cleaned on Excel.

American Energy Market Regulator

Tableau dashboards to visualizize data analyzing energy provider reliability metrics and illuminating pain points of outages.

American Energy Market Regulator SQL Project

Microsoft Excel & PowerPoint - revenue, cashflow, operational expenses analysis. Statistical analysis of product elasticity, pricing compared to market competitors. Regression analysis of mechanical failure points.

ChemCorp Project

Tableau dashboards of ChemCorp's customer base, annual trends, revenue analysis, profit margin and business recommendation to divest from low profit margin product.

COMING SOON
